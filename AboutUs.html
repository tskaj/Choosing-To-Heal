About Us

Zaeema Ahmad, PhD, RP
I hold a PhD. degree in Clinical Psychology and have been involved in the field of mental health (academics, research, and clinical practice) with +15 years of working experience in diverse settings including hospital, universities and private practice. I provide psychotherapy and counselling to adults, youth, and couples/families experiencing diverse range of challenges associated with mental health. My approach to therapy is client centered, collaborative, and trauma informed. Therapy is not ‘one size fits all’. I integrate a variety of modalities for treatment including Cognitive-behavior therapy, Mindfulness based therapies, Solution focused therapy, Dialectical Behavior therapy and Narrative approaches to understand your life experiences, develop and implement short and long-term goals, and help you meet your needs. I believe each individual is unique and has a capacity to grow to their fullest potential. I value providing a safe, compassionate and nonjudgmental environment, and support you to become aware of your feelings, thoughts and behaviors, identify obstacles, and work towards achieving your goals. I provide psychotherapy in English, Urdu and Hindi languages.
Riaz Ahmad, PhD, RP
Riaz Ahmad is a Registered Psychotherapist with the College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario (CRPO) and a member of Ontario Association of Mental Health Professionals (OAHMP). He holds a PhD. degree in Clinical Psychology and has extensive experience providing support to adults, youth, and couples/families experiencing depression, anxiety, trauma, grief, marital discord, verbal and emotional abuse, anger management, employee’s wellbeing, and other issues (More content will be added here). I provide psychotherapy in English, Urdu, Hindi and Punjabi languages.
