About Us

"Welcome to Choosing to Heal therapy services, where healing begins and lives are transformed. As a dedicated team of two psychologists, we are passionate about helping individuals, couples, and families navigate their mental health journeys. With our combined expertise and compassionate approach, we provide personalized therapy tailored to your unique needs. Our goal is to create a safe and nurturing space where you can explore your emotions, overcome challenges, and cultivate resilience. Utilizing evidence-based modalities such as cognitive-behavioral therapy, mindfulness techniques, and solution-focused therapy, we work collaboratively with you to achieve lasting positive change. We believe in the power of human connection and are committed to building a trusting therapeutic relationship with each client. Our aim is to empower you to uncover your strengths, gain insights, and develop effective coping strategies to enhance your well-being. Discover the transformative potential of therapy by joining us on this journey towards self-discovery and personal growth. Click the 'About Us' button below to learn more about our team and how we can support you."
